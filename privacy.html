Privacy Policy — Zoomies

Last updated: July 13, 2026

The short version

We don't collect your personal data. No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no trackers. The single exception: if you buy Premium, our payments partner processes an anonymous purchase record to validate your subscription — see Purchases below.

What data the app stores

Your tasks, steps, completed wins, streaks, focus-session history, and settings are stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers — we don't have servers. Nothing is shared with third parties, advertisers, or analytics companies. The app contains no ads, no trackers, and no analytics SDKs.

Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder or use the focus timer, notifications are scheduled locally on your device by the operating system. No notification data leaves your phone.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) and our payments partner RevenueCat, which processes an anonymous purchase identifier, your purchase history, and basic device information (such as device model and OS version) to validate your subscription. This data is not used for advertising or tracking and is never sold. We never see your name, email, or payment details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Data deletion

Your tasks and app data live on your device: deleting the app deletes them, and you can erase everything from inside the app via Settings → Erase all data. If you bought Premium and want the anonymous purchase record deleted too, email uplifebuilt@gmail.com and we'll remove it from our payments partner.

Children

The app is intended for users 13 and older and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if we add optional cloud backup), we will update this page and note it in the app's release notes before the change takes effect.
