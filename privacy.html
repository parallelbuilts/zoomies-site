Privacy Policy — Zoomies

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The short version

We don't collect your data. Any of it. Ever.

What data the app stores

Your tasks, steps, completed wins, streaks, focus-session history, and settings are stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers — we don't have servers. Nothing is shared with third parties, advertisers, or analytics companies. The app contains no ads, no trackers, and no analytics SDKs.

Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder or use the focus timer, notifications are scheduled locally on your device by the operating system. No notification data leaves your phone.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) and our payments partner RevenueCat, which processes an anonymous purchase identifier to validate your subscription. We never see your name, email, or payment details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Data deletion

Since all data lives on your device: deleting the app deletes your data. You can also erase everything from inside the app via Settings → Erase all data.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children, because it collects no personal information at all.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if we add optional cloud backup), we will update this page and note it in the app's release notes before the change takes effect.
